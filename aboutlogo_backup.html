The Story
Behind the Logo

Tvarová psychologie, Geometrie a Sémiotika

Logo není abstraktním symbolem, ale stylizovaným technickým nákresem (izometrickým řezem) základní stavební jednotky systému. Využívá principy Gestalt psychologie k tomu, aby mozek okamžitě dekódoval funkci a povahu produktu bez nutnosti číst text.

A Perfect Intersection

Our symbol sits at the intersection of two worlds. The sharp geometric lines represent the accuracy and modularity of modern flooring solutions, while the gradient flow signifies innovation and adaptability.

The shape itself—a stylized isometric tile—nods to both the physical product we install and the digital tools we use to design it. It represents a block of potential, ready to build something greater.

Precision

Like a pixel on a screen, every tile we place is positioned with absolute accuracy. There is no room for error in our grid.

Foundation

Floors are the foundation of every space. We build bases that are not only durable but also visually inspiring.

Innovation

We are constantly evolving. Our gradient represents the continuous flow of new ideas and technologies in our industry.
